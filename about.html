About Us

Preserving Ethiopian heritage through crafts.

Vision

Our vision is to empower Ethiopian artisans by providing them with opportunities to showcase their craftsmanship globally. Through our platform, we aim to amplify their voices and celebrate their unique cultural heritage. By fostering a thriving ecosystem of creativity and innovation, we envision a future where Ethiopian artistry is revered worldwide, contributing to sustainable economic growth and cultural exchange. We aspire to create a world where every handmade creation tells a story, connecting people across continents and preserving the rich tapestry of Ethiopia's artistic legacy for generations to come.

Mission

Our mission is multifaceted and deeply rooted in our commitment to preserving and promoting Ethiopia's rich cultural heritage. We endeavor to support local artisans and communities by creating sustainable livelihoods through fair trade practices. We strive to elevate the profile of Ethiopian craftsmanship on the global stage, fostering cross-cultural appreciation and understanding. Through collaborative partnerships and ethical business practices, we aim to empower artisans to thrive while safeguarding traditional techniques for future generations.

Goals

Our goals include:

  • Supporting local artisans and their communities through fair trade practices.
  • Creating economic opportunities and sustainable livelihoods for artisans.
  • Providing access to global markets to showcase Ethiopian craftsmanship.
  • Preserving traditional techniques and cultural heritage for future generations.
  • Fostering cross-cultural exchange and appreciation of Ethiopia's artistic traditions.
